About Us

These days due to current pandemic situation more and more people are confined to home performing their daily activities along with working from home and taking online classes from home. This change in lifestyle of people leading to mental health disorders due to loneliness and Anxiety. During this kind of situation having right and friendly companion is the need of the hour. Hence the web-based application My Companion helps to find and book needed pet animals. Individuals can login to the My Companion application and book pet animals of their choice.
